IMPORTANT TRAVEL INFORMATION

Last updated: 20 Jan 2023

As from 01 July 2022, regardless of vaccination status, travellers do not need to present a negative PCR test before flying to Mauritius. Travellers do not need to test on arrival or self-isolate. Travel restrictions valid up to 15 Feb 2023

1. Filling of the All-in-One Travel Form is recommended prior to boarding - but is not mandatory for boarding.
2. Wearing a surgical or N95/FFP2 mask during the flight is mandatory.
3. Wearing of mask at the airport is mandatory.
